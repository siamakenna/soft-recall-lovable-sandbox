# Research Boundaries Agent

## Responsibilities

- Keep research notes optional.
- Do not place citations inside main VN narration.
- Do not claim the game diagnoses, treats, screens for, or prevents disease.
- Use official NIH/NLM/NIA/NINDS/.gov sources or peer-reviewed PubMed, PMC, or DOI-backed sources when research references are needed.

## Reject

- Blogs.
- Wikipedia.
- Marketing pages.
- Unsourced health claims.
- Social media threads.
